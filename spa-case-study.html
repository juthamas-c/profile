Spa Booking Scheduler

Luxury spa booking experience designed with calm aesthetics, seamless flow, and intuitive mobile interaction. Let's take a look at some of the key features and designs below.

Key Features

Smart Scheduling

Real-time availability with intuitive date & time selection.

Therapist Profiles

View ratings, specialties, and experience before booking.

Secure Checkout

Fast and encrypted payment experience.

Booking Confirmation

Instant confirmation with appointment summary.

Problem & Strategy

Many boutique spas rely on manual booking coordination, leading to scheduling conflicts and revenue leakage. This project demonstrates how a digital booking flow can streamline therapist allocation, reduce no-shows, and enhance customer experience.
